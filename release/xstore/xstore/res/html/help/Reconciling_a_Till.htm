Reconciling a Till

The final check of a till is performed at till reconciliation, when the till is counted and checked against the expected amount in the till. Following till reconciliation, the till is closed.

Prerequisites: The following steps must be completed before you can reconcile a till:

  • The till must have an ENDCOUNT status. The associate has performed a close count on the till.
  • The till has been removed from the register. The associate has performed the Remove Till function at the register.
  • The till has been returned, indicating the till has been transferred from the control of the associate to the cash manager. The cash manager has marked the till as returned using the Issue/Return function.
  1. Select a till to reconcile from the list of tills, count the tenders at the Reconcile Count screen, and select Done Counting when finished.
  2. The system records the information, prints a reconcile receipt for your records, and returns to the Till Management menu.

Note: Deposits from the reconciled till are made to the store bank (safe).
