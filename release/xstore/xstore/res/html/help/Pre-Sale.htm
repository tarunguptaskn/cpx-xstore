Pre-Sale

Customer Lookup: Associating a Customer With the Sale

If a customer is not required for this transaction, select the Skip option to continue with the sale process. If you decide to associate a customer with this transaction later, select Customer Options and then choose the Assign Customer option on the Register Sale screen to display the Customer Lookup form.

When applicable, use the Change Country menu option to modify the drop-down State options and further refine your search. In the example below, the search is for Nancy Smith and the country has been changed to Canada so that the drop-down options contain Canadian Provinces (New Brunswick).

If the customer has a loyalty card, you may swipe it. You may also manually enter the customer’s number or name, loyalty number, or enter other customer information in the Customer Lookup form and select Process to continue.

Commissioned Associates

If your store tracks the associates’ sales for commissions and sales goals, you may be prompted to select a commissioned associate (or associates) to be linked to the sale transaction.

  1. If prompted, add a commissioned associate to the sale transaction through one of two methods:

Select the sale associate to be associated with this transaction from the list and press [Enter].

<OR>

Select the Select By Associate ID menu option to enter the associate’s ID and press [Enter].

  1. The system adds the associate to the sale transaction and the associates’ names are shown on the View Port.

Note: To assign a commissioned associate to an item during the sale, select Change Item --> Change Associates and follow the prompts.
