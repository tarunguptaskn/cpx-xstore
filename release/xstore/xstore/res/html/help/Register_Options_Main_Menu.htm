Register Options Main Menu

The Register Options here are primarily related to the movement of money into and out of a till. These are secured options and require a specific security level to access these functions.

Till Options include transactions such as Paid In and Paid Out, No Sale, Foreign Currency Maintenance, Tender Exchange, and Post Void.

Till Maintenance Options include transactions such as Cash Pickup, Cash Transfer, Till Audit, Mid-day deposit. The Till Accountability Mode functions included here are Issue/Return Till, Count Till, Attach Till, Remove Till Remove Other till, End Count Other Till, and Reconcile Till.

Store Bank Maintenance Options include functions for the control and auditing of funds in the store bank (safe) such as Store bank Audit, Open Store Bank (Till Accountability Mode), Reconcile Store Bank (Till Accountability Mode), Store Bank Cash Deposit. An option to prepare the Bank Deposit is also available.

Till Accountability Mode Functions

Some till options are available only if your system uses till accountability, in which responsibility for a till is assigned to a specific cashier. This also allows Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service to support more than one cash drawer on a single register. Till assignment is done through the Back Office Menu. Refer to the Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service Manager’s Guide for more information about all the functions available in Till Accountability mode.
