Issue a Till

Associates can only be assigned to one till at a time. The assigned till can be attached to any register the associate may work on during the day. The associate must count the assigned till and then attach the till to a cash drawer/register before ringing transactions.

The Till Details screen displays the current status of the tills:

Issued Column

  • A red X () indicates that the till has been returned to the cash office and is available for assignment.
  • A green check mark () indicates that the till has been issued to an associate.

Attached Column

  • A green check mark () indicates the associate has attached the till to a cash drawer and register.
  • A red X () indicates that the till is not attached to a cash drawer and register.

Count Status Column

  • UNCOUNTED - The assigned associate has not performed a begin count on the till.
  • BEGINCOUNT - The assigned associate has performed a begin count on the till.
  • ENDCOUNT - The assigned associate has performed an end count on the till. The till can be reconciled and placed back in circulation.
