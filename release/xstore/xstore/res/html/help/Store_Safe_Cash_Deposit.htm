Store Bank Cash Deposit

During the day, there may be times when you must deposit cash in the store bank. Use this procedure when a cash deposit is to be made into the store bank.

  1. The system prompts for the cash amount you are depositing into the store bank. Enter the amount, then press [Enter].
  2. Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service prints a receipt for the deposit and returns to the Store Bank Maintenance menu.
