Counting Inventory

If you do not have an inventory count document downloaded from the home office, you can create a new document at the store. Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service provides a number of different types of inventory counts to meet your particular requirements. An inventory count type must be selected when you decide to begin an inventory count.

  • Physical Inventory - Counting all in-stock items at a particular time.
  • Cycle Count - Counting a subset of the entire stock.
  • Lab Count - A specialized count of laboratory supplies only.
  • Supply Count - A specialized count of internally-used supplies, rather than saleable items.
  • Location-Based Inventory - Counting items that are limited to a defined inventory location.
  • Item-Based Inventory - Counting items on a list, regardless of their inventory locations.

Follow the on-screen prompts to create and find the count documents, create worksheets, and count the on-hand merchandise.

Refer to the Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service Shipping, Receiving, and Inventory Guide for detailed instructions.
