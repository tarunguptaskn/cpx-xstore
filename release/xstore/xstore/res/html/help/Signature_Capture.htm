Signature Capture

Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service displays the Signature Entry form while the customer signs the capture device attached to the register.

When the customer selects the Accept option on the signature capture device, Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service automatically displays the customer’s signature on this form so you can compare the captured signature with the customer’s signature on the credit card (or other document if applicable).

Press [Enter] if the signature matches the signature on the card. If the signature does not match, press [Esc] to return focus to the signature capture device and ask the customer to either write the signature again or choose to cancel rather than re-signing.

If the customer chooses the Cancel option, you have the option to Retry and ask the customer to sign on the signature capture device again, or to Skip the process and capture the customer’s signature manually on the receipt.

The Signature Entry form displays if the customer selects Cancel on the signature capture device.
