Opening the Store

The store open process must be performed on only one register. The store open procedure signals that the store is open to all registers. Once the store is open, sales cannot be rung until the register is opened and a till is counted for the register.

The Register Open and Store Open menu options toggle between open and close. The Store Open menu option indicates that this store is currently closed. The Register Open menu option indicates that this register is also closed.

  1. Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service prompts to confirm that you want to open the store. Select Yes to continue with the open store process.
  2. If prompted to confirm the business date, select Yes to confirm the date and continue with the open store process.

Note: If this date is incorrect, select No and you will be prompted to enter the new business date. Enter the date at the prompt. Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service prompts you to confirm the date you entered.

  1. Depending upon the configuration of your system, you may be prompted with other messages concerning the status of various functions within the store. Acknowledge these messages to continue with the store open process.

Note: that the store status indicator has changed from red to green indicating the store is now open. However, the Register status indicator is red indicating that this register is still closed.

  1. Select Yes at the prompt to open the register.
