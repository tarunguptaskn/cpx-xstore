Register Open: Cashier Activities

Note: The open/close processes include the counting of tills.

  1. When you attempt to log in to the system, Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service determines the status of the register and store. If the register status is closed, a Register Closed message may display in the message area of the register screen, if configured to do so.

The red register indicator on the status bar also shows that the register is closed.

  1. Select the Back Office option on the Register menu to open the register from the Back Office Main Menu.
  2. At the Back Office Main Menu, select the Open/Close Options option and press [Enter], or press the number associated with the menu option on the keyboard to display the Open/Close Options menu.
  3. At the Open/Close Options menu, select the Register Open option and press [Enter] or press the number associated with the menu option on the keyboard.
  4. At the register open confirmation prompt, press [Y] to confirm that you want to open this register.
  5. The system returns to the Open/Close Menu.
