Foreign Currency Maintenance

If permitted by your store’s policy, you can edit the currency exchange rate for the foreign currencies that are accepted as tenders for sale transactions. Since some currency rates fluctuate frequently, this feature allows you to edit the exchange rate as often as necessary.

Note: You may be prompted to verify your identity by entering your ID and password.
  1. At the foreign currency list, choose the foreign tender you want to edit.
  2. Enter the new exchange rate and a comment (optional), then select Save New Rate.
  3. After confirming that you want to complete the process, the new currency exchange rate is now effective.
