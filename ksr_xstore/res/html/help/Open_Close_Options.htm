Open/Close Options

The Register Close and Store Close menu options toggle between open and close. The register menu option Register Close indicates that the register  is open. The Store Close option tells you that the store is currently open.

  • Close Credit/Debit Batch: Requests a Close Batch from Merchantlink (a processor for credit/debit cards).
  • Changing the Business date: If no monetary transactions have been performed today, enter the new business date at the prompt. Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service prompts you to confirm the date you entered. The new business date will be used from this point on.
  • Force Close: If register equipment fails for any reason and a register cannot be closed using the normal procedure, the Force Close option may be used. By forcing a register to close, despite equipment failure, the store closing procedure may then be started.
  • The Print Till Summary Receipt: Allows you to print a summary of the tenders that were in the till when a register was closed. The receipt includes a breakdown of the various tenders (quantity and amount), a configurable list of various transactions that occurred at the register (paid in/paid out, returns, refunds, discounts, account setups, etc. and the quantity and amount for each) and taxes.
  • Check for Updates: Allows Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service to request the download manifest on demand. This option provides the ability to bypass the scheduler normally run during the store close, and to initiate the download manifest when called.

Refer to the Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service Manager's Guide for more information about these open/close processes.
