Cash Pickup

Your system’s configuration may specify the maximum amount that should be kept in a till. If that amount is exceeded after completing the tendering process for a transaction, the system will display a prompt indicating that a cash pickup should be made. However, you do not have to wait for this prompt to make a cash pickup.

Note: After you have assigned a customer to a transaction or added items to a sale, you cannot perform a cash pickup.
  1. You must be logged in to the system with your user ID and password.
  2. If your store is configured for foreign cash currency tenders to be given as change, select the type of tender from the list.
  3. At the Cash Pickup prompt, enter the cash amount that you are picking up and press [Enter].
  4. Remove the cash from the till and close the drawer. The system prints a receipt for this cash pickup transaction and returns to the Till Options menu. Select the Back option to return to the previous screen.
