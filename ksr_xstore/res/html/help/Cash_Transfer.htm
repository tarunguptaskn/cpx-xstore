Cash Transfer

A Cash Transfer is used to remove a specified amount of cash from the store bank (store safe) and move it into a till. The cash amount will be deducted from the store bank and added to the current amount in the till for accounting purposes.

To perform a Cash Transfer from the store bank to a till:

  1. If your store is configured for foreign cash currency tenders to be given as a change, select the type of tender from the list.
  2. At the prompt, enter the amount of cash to be transferred to the till, then press [Enter].
  3. A receipt prints for the cash transfer and Xstore returns to the Till Maintenance menu.
