Register Close

The register close process can be performed by store managers or associates who have the proper security privileges. A register close can be completed any time during the business day.

  1. From the register to be closed, select the Back Office option on the menu. Follow any prompts to log in to the Back Office as required.
  2. At the Back Office Main Menu, use the up and down arrow keys to select Open/Close Options and press [Enter] or press the number associated with the menu option on the keyboard.
  3. Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service displays the Open/Close Options menu. Use the up and down arrow keys to select the Register Close option and press [Enter] or press the number associated with the menu option on the keyboard.

Note: that the Register option toggles between open and close. If the option here is Register Close, the register is open. If the option here is Register Open, the register is closed.
  1. Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service displays a confirmation prompt to close the register. Select Yes to close the register.
  2. The cash drawer opens and Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service displays the Close Count Summary screen. Each count group (tender type) is counted individually. Specific groups require additional information as determined by the home office.

Organize your tenders prior to entering the count information to speed up the count/entry process. If you do not have any of the specific tender, skip that count group by pressing the Next Group option or by returning to the Close Count Summary screen.
