Locking and Unlocking a Register

Your system may be configured to automatically log out an associate after a specified period of time if there is no system activity. However, you may need to temporarily leave a register before that occurs. In that case, you can lock the register to prevent another person from using the system while you are away.

  • To lock the register, select the Lock Register option from the Register Options menu.

Note: Any functions that may still be available are determined by your store policy.
  • To unlock the register, enter your employee ID and press [Enter].

Note: The register may be unlocked only by the associate who locked it or someone who has sufficient privileges to unlock the register, such as a manager.
