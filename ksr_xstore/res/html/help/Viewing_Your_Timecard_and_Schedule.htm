Viewing Your Timecard and Schedule

View Timecard - Your timecard entries for the current payroll week are displayed in a view-only format; however, you can add a comment that will be linked to your timecard for this payroll week.

With your timecard record displayed, select the Timecard Comment option and type a comment in the Add Comment form that displays. The comment is now associated with this record as indicated by the See Comment text at the bottom of the Timecard Maintenance screen.

When you see an icon associated with a time clock record, it indicates the record has been entered or modified through Timecard Maintenance and is not the original clock-in or clock-out record, or that the record has an exception to a timecard rule.

View Work Schedule - Use this option to view and print your work schedule. The schedule information shown here includes the hours and your work assignment for each day of the week, and the total number of hours and break time scheduled for the week.

View Scheduled Employees - Use this option to view a list of associates who are scheduled to work on a specific day. The information shown here includes each associate’s name, work code, and their scheduled shift hours.

Note: To view your timecard/schedule for a different week, select the Payroll Dates or Schedule Dates option and select a week from the list.
