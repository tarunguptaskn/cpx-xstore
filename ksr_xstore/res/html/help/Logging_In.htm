Logging In

No transactions can be entered into Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service until you log in at the Register Login screen. This prevents unauthorized personnel from accessing the register. Logging in also allows the system to record each associate’s sales, calculate commissions, and compare sales to employee goals.

  1. To log in, scan or key your employee ID number and press [Enter].
  2. When prompted, type your password and press [Enter].

Biometric Fingerprint Scans

If your system is set up to use a Biometric Fingerprint device, scan your fingerprint rather than entering your user ID and password. You will not be prompted to enter your password if you scanned your finger. Keyed entry and card scan are also supported.

You may be able to access several functions in Oracle Retail Xstore Point of Service without being logged in to the register. For example, you may be able to perform item lookups and to view your work schedule before logging in. The available functions are determined by the home office and your store policy.
